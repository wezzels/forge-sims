# boost-intercept — Boost-Phase Intercept

## Purpose
Models boost-phase intercept attempts (airborne laser, kinetic intercept) during missile boost.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Boost phase: 180-300 second window depending on threat
- Kinetic intercept from airborne platforms
- ABL (Airborne Laser) engagement modeling
- Very short engagement windows
- Requires proximity to launch site

### Scenarios
`abl-engagement`, `kinetic-boost-intercept`, `boost-window-analysis`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Boost phase timing, engagement geometry, short windows
**Needs improvement**: ABL beam propagation, atmospheric turbulence, platform positioning logistics
