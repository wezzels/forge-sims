# population-impact-sim — Population Impact Assessment

## Purpose
Models the human impact of nuclear/ballistic missile strikes on population centers.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Nuclear yield modeling (thermal, blast, radiation)
- Population density data for major cities
- Casualty estimation (killed, injured, displaced)
- Fallout plume modeling with wind direction

### Scenarios
`icbm-city-strike`, `mrvs-multi-city`, `regional-strike`, `full-scale`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Nuclear effects modeling, population density data
**Needs improvement**: Real census data integration, fallout modeling simplified, no evacuation modeling
