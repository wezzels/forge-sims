# bmd-sim-thaad-er — THAAD Extended Range

## Purpose
Models the proposed THAAD-ER with extended range and altitude capability.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Extended range booster for THAAD
- Range: ~300+ km, altitude up to 200+ km
- Improved PK at extended ranges
- Same hit-to-kill philosophy as THAAD

### Scenarios
`thaad-er-vs-irbm`, `thaad-er-vs-hgv`, `extended-range-engagement`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Based on THAAD physics with extended parameters
**Needs improvement**: Still proposed system — parameters are estimates, not validated
