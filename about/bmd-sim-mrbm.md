# bmd-sim-mrbm — Medium-Range Ballistic Missile

## Purpose
Models MRBM threats (DF-21, Iskander-M, etc.) with 1,000-3,000 km range.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Single/two-stage boost
- Shorter flight times (8-15 minutes)
- Terminal maneuvering variants
- Lower apogee than IRBM/ICBM

### Scenarios
`df-21d`, `iskander-m`, `mrbm-raid`, `maneuvering-mrbm`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Basic trajectory physics, terminal guidance
**Needs improvement**: Detailed maneuver profiles, conventional warhead effects, TBM vs MRBM distinction
