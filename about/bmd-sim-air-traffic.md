# bmd-sim-air-traffic — Air Traffic for BMD Discrimination

## Purpose
Models civilian air traffic for BMDS false alarm reduction and commercial aircraft discrimination.

## Physics & Fidelity
- ADS-B/Mode S transponder modeling
- Flight corridor density analysis by region
- Correlation of air tracks with BMDS sensor returns
- False alarm reduction through track correlation

### Scenarios
`normal-traffic`, `high-density`, `corridor-analysis`

## Accuracy: Low-Medium
**Reliable**: Basic air traffic density model
**Needs improvement**: Real flight data, dynamic routing, weather diversions
