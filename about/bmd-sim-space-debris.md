# bmd-sim-space-debris — Space Debris Risk

## Purpose
Models space debris environment and collision risk for BMDS satellites.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Debris population modeling (trackable vs. non-trackable)
- Collision probability calculation
- Kessler syndrome scenario modeling
- Debris cloud evolution after interception

### Scenarios
`debris-field`, `collision-risk`, `keessler-syndrome`, `post-intercept-debris`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Debris population statistics, collision probability calculation
**Needs improvement**: Real USSPACECOM catalog data, long-term evolution modeling, conjunction filtering
