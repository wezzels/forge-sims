# debris-field — Post-Intercept Debris Field

## Purpose
Models debris field generation and risk after kinetic intercept.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Fragment generation from hit-to-kill and blast-frag intercepts
- Debris cloud expansion modeling
- Collision risk for operational satellites
- Long-term debris evolution

### Scenarios
`ekv-intercept`, `sm3-intercept`, `thaad-intercept`, `cascade-risk`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Fragment generation models, cloud expansion, risk assessment
**Needs improvement**: Detailed fragmentation modeling, orbital perturbation, long-term evolution
