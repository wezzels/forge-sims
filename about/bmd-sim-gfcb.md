# bmd-sim-gfcb — Global Force Coordination Board

## Purpose
Models the global force coordination and resource allocation for BMDS assets.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Asset allocation across theaters
- Sensor-weapon pairing optimization
- Resource constraint modeling (limited interceptors, radar time)
- Theater prioritization and deconfliction

### Scenarios
`global-allocation`, `theater-priority`, `resource-constraint`, `multi-theater`

## Accuracy: Medium
**Reliable**: Resource allocation logic, sensor-weapon pairing
**Needs improvement**: Real C2BMC integration details, coalition coordination, ROE variation by theater
