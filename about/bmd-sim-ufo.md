# bmd-sim-ufo — Unknown Anomalous Phenomena

## Purpose
Models UFO/UAP threats with physics-violating characteristics for testing BMDS response to unconventional threats.

## Physics & Fidelity
- Physics-violating maneuvers (instantaneous acceleration, hypersonic zigzag)
- Radar signatures that don't match any known threat category
- Intermittent detection (appears/disappears)
- No propulsion thermal signature
- BMDS radars attempt detection/track with severely degraded PK

### Scenarios
`tic-tac`, `gimbal`, `go-fast`, `transmedium`

## Accuracy: N/A (intentionally unrealistic)
**Purpose**: Stress-test BMDS against edge-case threats that violate known physics
**Limitations**: By design, not physically accurate — used for system resilience testing
